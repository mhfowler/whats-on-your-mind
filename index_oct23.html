To download the Facebook Scraper app,
  1. Click the download link below (it will download a .zip file).
  2. Double click Facebook Scraper.zip to unzip it.
  3. Inside the unzipped folder is a mac application as well as instructions on how to use it.
Download
The Facebook Scraper app helps you download the Facebook statuses of all of your Facebook friends from the week after November 8, 2016 and combines them together into a PDF in chronological order.
This tool was developed by Max Fowler and Rachel Haberstroh to help find statuses for What's On Your Mind, a publication collecting writing posted on Facebook in the week after the November 8, 2016 election.
If you don't have a Mac, and would like to use the scraper, send an email to maxhfowler@gmail.com and we can show you how to run it on Windows or Linux.
